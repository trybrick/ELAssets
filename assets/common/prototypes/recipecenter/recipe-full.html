Grilled Steak with Sauteed Mushrooms

Rated by 10 users
Prep time:
30 minutes
Cook time:
45 minutes
Total time:
1 hour 15 minutes
Serves:
4

If you want a really great steak for the barbecue grill, be sure to pick a cut with a good bit of marbling. Fat and age make for a great grilled steak!

  • Check All
  • Add Checked
  • Add All
  • Trimmed 1-inch-thick strip sirloin, club or filet mignon steaks
  • Peppercorns
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 oz. brandy, optional
  1. Use: Trimmed 1-inch-thick strip sirloin, club or filet mignon steaks
  2. Crush don't grind: Peppercorns coarsely on a board with a pressing, rolling movement, using the bottom of a pan.
  3. Press the steaks into the crushed pepper and work it into both sides of the meat with the heel of your palm or with the flat side of a cleaver.
  4. Sprinkle the bottom of a 9-inch skillet with: 2 teaspoons salt
  5. When it begins to brown, put the steaks into the pan and brown uncovered over high heat.
  6. Reduce to medium heat, turn steaks and cook to desired degree of rareness. In a separate pan, prepare:
    • 1/4 cup butter
    • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
    • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  7. Remove steaks from the pan in which they have been cooked and discard the pan drippings. If you wish to flambe the steaks, page 155, do so with: (2 oz. brandy)
  8. Serve the butter mixture separately.
