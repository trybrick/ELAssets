Lamb Chops with Aspargus Spears

Serving Size: 5 oz.

Servings Scale

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 1lb fresh Asparagus, ends cut
Made {24} times

This meal makes a great dinner for two, and is surprisingly easy to make. Wow your next date with your ”advanced” cooking skills with this recipe.

Cooking Instructions

In a cast-iron pan (or non-stick), set to medium-high heat and melt 1 tbsp of butter until melted. Coat lamb chops in a dry rub with coriander, cumin, and salt and pepper to taste. Add lamb chops to the pan, cooking until brown on both sides (about 5 minutes per side). Remove lamb chops and set aside.

Add red wine and broth to the pan to deglaze the pan. Cook off excess liquid untill you have a thick sauce. Drizzle sauce over lamb chops on a serving tray.

In a separate skillet, heat the rest of the butter until melted over medium heat. Add the aspargus and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Cook until apargus is bright green and well done, but not mushy. Serve immediately. Serves 4.
